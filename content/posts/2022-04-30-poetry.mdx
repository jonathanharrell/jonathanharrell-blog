> I let an oar drop.<br/>
> I know how it looks.<br/>
> And I see your shock.<br/>
> Like you saw a face through a dark river.<br/>
> To you I'm suddenly speaking Gaelic,<br/>
> like language translated<br/>
> into slow light –<br/>
> and swift dark –
> <cite>Gaelic, by Jen Hadfield (excerpt)</cite>
