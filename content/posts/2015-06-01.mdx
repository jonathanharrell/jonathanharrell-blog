I had a dream,<br/>
That I was up amongst the clouds.<br/>
I could see storms forming,<br/>
Roiling cumulonimbus,<br/>
Darkening forests, fields, and plains.<br/>
I could see the sun, too,<br/>
Until the clouds covered it<br/>
And the air turned cold.
