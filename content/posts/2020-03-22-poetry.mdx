> We live on a hillside<br/>
> close to water<br/>
> We eat in darkness<br/>
> We sleep in the coldest<br/>
> part of the house<br/>
> We love in silence<br/>
> We keep our poetry<br/>
> locked in a glass cabinet<br/>
> Some nights We stay up<br/>
> passing it back and<br/>
> forth<br/>
> between us<br/>
> drinking deep
> <cite>What Keeps, by C.D. Wright</cite>
